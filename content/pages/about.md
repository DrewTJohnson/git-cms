# About Us

## Our Story

Founded in 2010, our organization began as a small group of passionate volunteers who believed that everyone deserves access to quality resources and opportunities. What started as weekly community meetings has grown into a full-service non-profit serving hundreds of individuals every year.

## Our Team

We are powered by a dedicated board of directors, a small full-time staff, and over 50 active volunteers who give their time and energy to our cause.

## Our Values

**Inclusivity** — We welcome everyone, regardless of background, ability, or circumstance.

**Transparency** — We believe in open communication with our donors, volunteers, and community.

**Impact** — We measure our success by the real, tangible difference we make in people's lives.
